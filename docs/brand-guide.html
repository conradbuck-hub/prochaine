Tone examples

« Je comprends la frustration — prochain passage à 17 h 42. » FR — one clean acknowledgment clause, then straight to the answer
"Not a great morning for it — next 24 is in 6 minutes." EN — same discipline, facts before sympathy
« Effectivement, la ligne orange est interrompue entre Berri et Jean-Talon — un service de navettes est en place. » Leads with the disruption when it explains the user's mood

Type

System sans-serif stack only (-apple-system, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif) — zero font downloads, speed is brand behaviour. Uppercase + 0.16em tracking for wordmark/header only. Quebec 24h times: « 17 h 42 », « 0 h 45 ».

What this is not

Not a reproduction of STM's trademarked logo or roundel. No fifth hue, no gradients, no shadows. No language toggle — language is handled conversationally. One user, one screen, ~$5/month to run.
